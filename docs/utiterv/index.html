Operational Notes

  • Grand Canyon: park early and use shuttles; do not move the RV around the rim all day.
  • Grand Canyon hike: South Kaibab to Cedar Ridge is the default. Skeleton Point is only for a cool dawn start and strong conditions.
  • Route 66: Sitgreaves Pass is paved and feasible in a mid-size Cruise America RV, but it is narrow, twisty, and not a bad-weather road.
  • Yosemite: Tioga Pass is the key dependency. If it closes, reroute around the Sierra via Bakersfield/Fresno and keep Yosemite from the west side.
  • Service rhythm: use Ely, Lees Ferry/Page, Grand Canyon/Williams, Las Vegas/Pahrump, and Lone Pine/Bishop for dump/refill/laundry as needed.
